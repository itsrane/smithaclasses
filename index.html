Our Educational Journey

A Structured Approach to Growth

1. Pre-Primary Wing

(Jr. KG & Sr. KG)

  • Play-based Learning & Sensory Integration.
  • Early Literacy and Numeracy Skills.
  • Focus on Fine Motor Skills and Social-Emotional Development.
  • Small teacher-to-student ratio for personalized care.

2. Primary School

(Grades 1 to 5)

  • Core Subject Mastery (English, Math, Science, Social Studies).
  • Introduction to Critical Thinking and Problem Solving.
  • Hands-on learning through activity-based projects.
  • Emphasis on curiosity and foundation building.

3. Secondary School

(Grades 6 to 10)

  • Advanced Academic Rigor and Examination Preparation.
  • Specialized Electives (Science, Commerce, Arts introduction).
  • Career Guidance and Leadership Development.
  • Focus on independent research and analytical skills.
